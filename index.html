Explorations


While no project is ever truly complete, these are some things I'm actively pursuing and trying to improve.


Colon Cancer Detection

Current prescreening methods are either sensitive and expensive or cheap and lacking in sensitivity. I used artificial intelligence to come up with a cheap yet sensitive approach to prescreening for colorectal cancer. I'm currently trying to consolidate it all into one device and make that device reusable for multiple tests.

Retinal Examinations

I was able to come up with feature extraction algorithms in combination with ensemble ML methods that could boost AI-based diabetic retinopathy prescreening sensitivities to 96%. I'm trying to improve on this by creating a cheaper retinal imaging device.
