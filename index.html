About Me

I am an all-round developer specializing in web development. I do my back-end work with Node.js, Rails, or Laravel with MySQL, Sqlite3, or MongoDB. I do my front-end work in HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. I am currently working on cross-platform app development using Apache Cordova.


Apart from developing, I am a pentalingual polyglot, if I say so myself (that's English, Latin, Sanskrit, Attic Greek, and my family's language of Konkani). I have won multiple awards in STEM competitions such as scoring in the 85th percentile of the AMC Math Test for two years, and medaling multiple times in Science Olympiad and TSA. I am also a gold medalist in the National Latin Exam, have won the State championships in Certamen (a Latin trivia game), and have acheived first place in the state for Latin Sight Reading.
